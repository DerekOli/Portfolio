How many memory cards do I need?
Most games save progress using a single memory card, but it’s recommended to have at least two for maximum storage.
Do PS1 games support multiplayer?
Yes! Many games support two players via the controller ports, and some use the Multitap accessory for up to four players.
Can I use my music CDs in the playstation?
Yes! The PS1 can play standard audio CDs, letting you enjoy your favorite music.
Are DualShock controllers necessary?
Not strictly, but they provide vibration feedback and analog controls for a more immersive experience. On the rare occasion a game does require the use of a dualshock controller These games will be labeled as such on the box art. For a full list, see the PS1 Analog Support List.
What display options are available?
The PS1 outputs standard composite video, S-Video, or component video. For an in-depth technical breakdown of PS1 video output options, check out the RetroRGB PS1 Blog.

PS1 vs N64 Storage Capacity

While Nintendo 64 cartridges can ONLY hold up to 64 MB, a single PlayStation 1 disc can store up to 700 MB of data; over ten times more! This extra space allows for full-motion video, CD-quality music, and larger 3D environments.
